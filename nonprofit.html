Why This Matters To Me

Finance decides which programs exist: Can we run one more table tennis session for seniors with Parkinson’s? Can we afford homemade Bengali fish at the next event? Can we make sure teenagers understand debt before they sign something that will follow them for a decade?

My work is about teaching, resourcing and reducing gatekeeping. Knowing financial literacy should be a right, and using money properly is a moral obligation.
